Welcome to the Kappa Kappa Psi - Tau Beta Sigma Prospective Membership Tool!

This tool was designed with new Candidates in mind, so that you may easily obtain a wealth of knowledge regarding the Candidate process in one simple location, regardless of your affiliation!


    One can use this tool to:
  • Study the History of the Fraternity or Sorority
  • Learn more about the Active Chapters
  • Track Interviews quickly and easily
